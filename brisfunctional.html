BrisFunctional

Things you might want to know:

  • An interest in functional programming is the only thing we'll ask
  • You don't need to be an expert, or even have any experience at all.
  • We're language-agnostic, typically we've used Haskell, Scala, Clojure, Groovy, etc but if you know something else...
  • ...We're always looking for new ideas, topics & speakers.
  • We're doing a mixture of practical stuff (looking at Project Euler, L-Systems for example) and some show-n-tell sessions as well.
  • We might have 3 or 4 speakers in an evening, but we'll always try to include some coding
  • Bring laptops if you like, there is wifi. There is also pizza.
  • We meet roughly monthly, on a Tuesday.
